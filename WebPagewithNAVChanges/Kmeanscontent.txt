

K means definition

Clusters are group of similar items. Similar with respect to know features. 
This is a kind of classifier learning alternative to Supervised learnin
In k-means, there can k clusters each clusters representing features of same type.
No two clusters are similar. 
Each cluster is determined by center point, whose nearest distance from given test point is used as measure of selecting a group
K means is a two part iterative algorithm. First part uses the output of  second part to further improve condition.
